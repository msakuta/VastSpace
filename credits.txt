The Saturn Texture
------------------

The Saturn texture is made by Bjorn Jonsson and obtained from:
http://www.mmedia.is/~bjj/planetary_maps.html



M16 Assault Rifle's Gunshot Sound (Public Domain)
-------------------------------------------------

m16-1.ogg, m16-2.ogg, m16-3.ogg are retrieved from

http://www.freesound.org/people/qubodup/sounds/187677/

and edited to fit our project's use.


Sniper Rifle's Gunshot Sound (Public Domain)
-------------------------------------------------

sniper-1.ogg, sniper-2.ogg are retrieved from

http://www.freesound.org/people/qubodup/sounds/182790/

and edited to fit our project's use.


Ceres Texture
-------------

Obtained from NASA (public domain):

http://solarsystem.nasa.gov/multimedia/gallery/Ceres_optimized.jpg

and edited to fit this application as a dwarf plant texture.
Namely, cropping, mirror repeating, local contrast normalization and
contrast/brightness tweak is applied.
